Selection Procedure

Interested candidates are required to complete and submit the online registration form, followed by an in-person interview round. Shortlisting will be done after each stage of the selection process.

General Eligibility Criteria:

  • Full-time enrollment in any program at GJUS&T is required.
  • Active participation in all team activities and initiatives is expected.
  • Openness to learning, personal growth, and handling responsibilities is essential.
  • Consistent availability for meetings and team commitments is necessary.
