What is iConnect??


Team iConnect is a vibrant student organization operating under the Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay Innovation & Incubation Center (PDUIIC) at Guru Jambheshwar University of Science and Technology (GJUS&T) in Hisar. We aim to nurture innovation, promote collaboration, and enhance skill development.


iConnect is a dynamic platform for students to explore their potential and contribute to groundbreaking advancements. iConnect is supported by a well-structured administrative team, an experienced advisory panel, and a robust mentoring system, ensuring comprehensive guidance and assistance for its members.


We are dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical learning and practical application through workshops, hackathons, seminars, and various projects. Aligned with the vision of PDUIIC, iConnect strives to cultivate a culture of excellence and sustainable development, making it a cornerstone for aspiring innovators and changemakers within the university and beyond.
