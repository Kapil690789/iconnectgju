Let’s see, what Konark 2.O is all about?

Konark 2.O 2025: Get Ready for an Unforgettable Experience!

Organized by iConnect Society under the Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay Innovation & Incubation Centre at Guru Jambheshwar University of Science & Technology, the Konark 2.O 2025 promises to be a hub of innovation, entrepreneurship, and inspiration. Whether you're an aspiring entrepreneur, a seasoned business leader, or someone passionate about innovation, this event has something for everyone.

Event Highlights

Interactive Sessions

Participate in hands-on workshops and interactive sessions designed to enhance your skills and knowledge. Learn from experts in fields such as marketing, finance, product development, and more. These sessions will provide practical takeaways that you can apply to your own entrepreneurial journey.

Thrilling Competitions

Showcase your talents and compete in various competitions tailored for innovators and entrepreneurs. Whether it's a pitch contest, hackathon, or a business plan competition, there are opportunities to win rewarding prizes and gain recognition.

Rewarding Prizes

Exciting prizes await the winners of our competitions. From cash awards to mentorship opportunities and incubation support, these prizes are designed to help you take your ideas to the next level.

Fun Games

Relax and unwind with fun and engaging games. Network with peers in a casual setting, challenge your friends, and enjoy the lighter side of the summit.

Networking Opportunities

Meet and connect with industry leaders, successful entrepreneurs, investors, and like-minded peers. This is a great chance to expand your professional network, find potential collaborators, and gain valuable contacts that could benefit your entrepreneurial journey.

Don’t Miss Out!

Konark 2.O 2025 is your opportunity to be part of something extraordinary. Join us to learn, compete, network, and have fun. Get ready for an experience that could transform your future.

For more information and updates, follow us on our social media channels and visit our official event website. We can't wait to see you there!
